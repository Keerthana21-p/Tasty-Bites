  1. Mix all marinade ingredients in a bowl.

  1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan. Cook marinated chicken until done and slightly charred.
  2. Set aside and retain the pan for gravy.

  6. Add the cooked chicken and stir gently to coat in sauce.

  • Garnish with chopped coriander and a swirl of cream.
  • Serve hot with naan, roti, or steamed basmati rice.
