🥦 Broccoli & Tofu Stir Fry
🧂 Ingredients
🍲 For the stir-fry:
  • 200g firm tofu (pressed and cubed)
  • 1½ cups broccoli florets
  • 1 small bell pepper (optional, sliced)
  • 2 tbsp oil (sesame or vegetable)
  • 1 tsp garlic (minced)
  • ½ tsp ginger (minced)
  • 1–2 spring onions (chopped, white and green separated)
🥣 For the sauce:
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce (low sodium preferred)
  • 1 tbsp oyster sauce (or hoisin sauce for vegan)
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar or white vinegar
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce (like sriracha, optional)
  • 1 tsp sugar or honey (optional)
  • 1 tsp cornflour + 2 tbsp water (slurry)
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Prep the tofu
  • Press tofu for 15–20 minutes to remove excess water.
  • Cut into cubes and pat dry.
  • Optional: Toss in a little cornflour and salt for crisp texture.
  • Pan-fry in 1 tbsp oil until golden and crispy. Set aside.
🔸 Step 2: Stir-fry the vegetables
  • Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or pan.
  • Add garlic, ginger, and spring onion whites. Sauté 30 seconds.
  • Add broccoli and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
  • Add bell peppers and cook till veggies are tender but crisp.
🔸 Step 3: Prepare and add the sauce
  • In a bowl, mix all sauce ingredients including cornflour slurry.
  • Pour sauce into the pan, stir, and let it simmer until thickened.
🔸 Step 4: Combine & serve
  • Add the fried tofu back into the pan.
  • Toss gently to coat with the sauce.
  • Garnish with spring onion greens.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

💚 This recipe is protein-rich, fiber-filled, and perfect for a quick, nutritious dinner!

🥣🍌 Oats & Banana Smoothie Bowl
🧂 Ingredients
🍌 For the smoothie base:
  • 1 ripe banana (frozen or fresh)
  • ½ cup rolled oats (soaked for 10 mins optional)
  • ½ cup milk (dairy or plant-based)
  • ¼ cup plain yogurt (or plant-based yogurt)
  • 1–2 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • ½ tsp cinnamon (optional)
  • 1–2 ice cubes (optional)
🍓 Topping ideas (customize as you like):
  • Sliced bananas
  • Fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Chia seeds or flaxseeds
  • Granola
  • Sliced almonds, walnuts, or cashews
  • Peanut butter or almond butter (drizzled)
  • Coconut flakes
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Make the smoothie base
  • In a blender, add banana, oats, milk, yogurt, cinnamon, and sweetener.
  • Blend until thick and creamy.
  • Add more milk for thinner or more oats for thicker texture.
🔸 Step 2: Pour and top
  • Pour the smoothie into a shallow bowl.
  • Decorate with toppings in rows or sections for a beautiful finish.
🔸 Step 3: Serve
  • Enjoy immediately while chilled and fresh!

💡 This smoothie bowl is rich in fiber, potassium, and energy — great for breakfast or post-workout refuel!

🍗🥦 Grilled Chicken with Steamed Vegetables
🧂 Ingredients
🍗 For the grilled chicken:
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp garlic (minced)
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • ½ tsp paprika (or red chili powder)
  • ½ tsp dried herbs (oregano, thyme, or Italian herbs)
  • Salt to taste
🥦 For the steamed vegetables:
  • ½ cup broccoli florets
  • ½ cup carrot slices
  • ½ cup green beans
  • ½ cup bell peppers (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp olive oil or butter (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice or vinegar (optional)
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Marinate the chicken
  • Mix olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, pepper, paprika, herbs, and salt in a bowl.
  • Coat the chicken breasts and marinate for at least 15–30 minutes (or up to 6 hours in the fridge).
🔸 Step 2: Grill the chicken
  • Option 1 (Grill): Preheat grill to medium and cook chicken for 6–7 minutes per side or until done.
  • Option 2 (Pan): Heat 1 tsp oil in a nonstick pan and cook until golden and cooked through.
  • Let rest for 5 minutes, then slice.
🔸 Step 3: Steam the vegetables
  • Steam broccoli, carrots, green beans, and optional veggies for 5–6 minutes.
  • Toss with salt, pepper, lemon juice, and olive oil (if using).
🔸 Step 4: Serve
  • Plate grilled chicken slices with steamed vegetables on the side.
  • Optionally, serve with brown rice or quinoa.

✅ This dish is high in protein and fiber, making it perfect for a healthy, balanced meal.

🥗🌱 Sprouted Moong Chaat Recipe
🧂 Ingredients
  • 1 cup sprouted green moong (mung beans)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (finely chopped)
  • ½ cucumber (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional, finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  • ½ tsp chaat masala
  • ½ tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (or to taste)
  • 1 tbsp pomegranate seeds (optional)
  • 1 tbsp sev or crushed papdi (optional, for crunch)
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Prepare the sprouts
  • Rinse and soak whole green moong overnight.
  • Drain and keep in a clean cloth or sprouter for 1–2 days until sprouted.
  • Optionally steam for 2–3 minutes for softness and easy digestion.
🔸 Step 2: Mix the chaat
  • In a bowl, combine moong sprouts, onion, tomato, cucumber, and green chili.
  • Add salt, chaat masala, cumin powder, and lemon juice.
  • Toss well to mix flavors.
🔸 Step 3: Serve
  • Garnish with coriander, pomegranate seeds, sev, or crushed papdi.
  • Serve immediately as a healthy snack or light lunch.

✅ High in protein, fiber, and vitamins — great for weight loss and digestion!

🥣🌿 Vegetable Clear Soup Recipe
🧂 Ingredients
  • 1 tsp oil or butter
  • 2–3 garlic cloves (finely chopped)
  • ½ inch ginger (grated or finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp spring onion whites (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp carrots (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp cabbage (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp beans (finely chopped)
  • 2 tbsp capsicum or bell pepper (optional)
  • 3 cups water or vegetable stock
  • Salt to taste
  • ¼ tsp crushed black pepper
  • 1 tsp soy sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)
  • 1 tbsp spring onion greens or coriander – for garnish
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Sauté vegetables
  • Heat oil or butter in a deep pan.
  • Add garlic, ginger, and spring onion whites. Sauté until aromatic.
  • Add carrots, cabbage, beans, and bell pepper. Stir-fry for 2–3 mins over medium-high heat.
🔸 Step 2: Simmer the soup
  • Add 3 cups of water or vegetable stock.
  • Bring to a boil, then simmer for 8–10 mins.
  • Add salt, black pepper, and soy sauce (if using). Mix well and taste test.
🔸 Step 3: Finish & Serve
  • Stir in lemon juice for a fresh kick (optional).
  • Turn off the flame and garnish with spring onion greens or coriander.
  • Serve hot with toasted bread or as a warm starter!

✅ Light, warming, and full of immune-boosting nutrients. Perfect for detox or a light meal!

🥗🌱 Chickpea Salad Recipe
🧂 Ingredients
  • 1½ cups cooked or canned chickpeas (drained and rinsed)
  • ½ cup cucumber (chopped)
  • ½ cup tomatoes (chopped, deseeded)
  • ¼ cup red onion (finely chopped)
  • ¼ cup bell peppers (any color, chopped)
  • 2 tbsp fresh coriander or parsley (chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional, finely chopped)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1–2 tsp lemon juice (adjust to taste)
  • ½ tsp roasted cumin powder
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 tbsp feta cheese, olives, or avocado chunks
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Prepare the Chickpeas
  • Rinse and drain canned chickpeas well.
  • If using dry chickpeas, soak overnight and boil until soft, then cool.
🔸 Step 2: Mix the Salad
  • In a large bowl, combine chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, onion, bell peppers, and herbs.
  • Add olive oil, lemon juice, cumin powder, salt, and pepper.
  • Toss gently until everything is well coated.
🔸 Step 3: Garnish and Serve
  • Chill the salad for 20 minutes to enhance flavors (optional).
  • Garnish with feta, avocado, or toasted nuts/seeds.
  • Serve fresh as a snack, starter, or light meal.

✅ High-protein, fiber-rich, and perfect for a refreshing, healthy bite.

🌶️🍚 Stuffed Bell Peppers Recipe (Vegetarian)

🧂 Ingredients

🫑 For the peppers:
  • 4 large bell peppers (any color)
  • 1 tbsp oil (for brushing or baking)
🥣 For the stuffing:
  • 1 cup cooked rice or quinoa
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tomato (chopped)
  • ½ cup corn kernels (boiled or frozen)
  • ½ cup cooked black beans or chickpeas (optional)
  • ½ cup paneer or cheese (crumbled or grated) – optional
  • 1 tsp garlic (minced)
  • ½ tsp cumin seeds or powder
  • ½ tsp paprika or chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp chopped coriander or parsley
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
🧀 Optional toppings:
  • Grated cheese (cheddar, mozzarella)
  • Chili flakes
  • Crushed tortilla chips (for crunch)

👨‍🍳 Instructions

🔸 Step 1: Prep the bell peppers
  • Cut the tops off and remove seeds and membranes.
  • Brush inside and out with oil.
  • Pre-bake at 180°C (350°F) for 10 minutes to soften (optional but improves texture).
🔸 Step 2: Make the stuffing
  • Heat oil in a pan, sauté onion and garlic until soft.
  • Add tomatoes, corn, beans and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  • Add rice, spices, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  • Turn off heat. Add lemon juice and fresh herbs. Cool slightly.
  • Mix in paneer or cheese (optional).
🔸 Step 3: Stuff and bake
  • Fill each bell pepper generously with stuffing.
  • Top with cheese (if using).
  • Place in baking dish and bake at 180°C (350°F) for 20–25 mins until golden and tender.
🔸 Step 4: Serve
  • Garnish with fresh herbs, yogurt, or dip.
  • Serve hot with salad, soup, or garlic bread.
🍧🍓 Greek Yogurt with Fruits & Nuts

🧂 Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt (plain or lightly sweetened)
  • ½ banana (sliced)
  • ¼ cup fresh berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 tbsp almonds (chopped or slivered)
  • 1 tbsp walnuts or pistachios (chopped)
  • 1 tsp chia seeds or flaxseeds (optional)
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup (optional, for sweetness)
  • A dash of cinnamon (optional)

👨‍🍳 Instructions:

🔸 Step 1: Prepare the base
  • In a bowl, add Greek yogurt.
  • If desired, stir in honey or maple syrup for added sweetness.
🔸 Step 2: Add fruits
  • Top with sliced banana, berries, or seasonal chopped fruits like mango, kiwi, or apple.
🔸 Step 3: Add crunch
  • Sprinkle chopped nuts and seeds over the fruits.
  • Add a dash of cinnamon for extra flavor (optional).
🔸 Step 4: Serve
  • Serve immediately as a breakfast bowl, healthy snack, or post-workout meal.
