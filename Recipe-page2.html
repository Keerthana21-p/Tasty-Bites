🍗 Kung Pao Chicken Recipe

🧂 Ingredients
🐓 For the Chicken:
  • 300g boneless chicken breast or thigh (cut into cubes)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Shaoxing wine (or rice vinegar)
  • ½ tsp cornflour
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¼ tsp pepper
🥣 For the Sauce:
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar (or white vinegar)
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (optional)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 2 tsp cornflour
  • 4 tbsp water
🍳 For Stir-Frying:
  • 2 tbsp oil (peanut or vegetable)
  • 8–10 dried red chilies (broken, seeds removed)
  • 1 tbsp Sichuan peppercorns (optional)
  • 4–5 garlic cloves (chopped)
  • 1-inch ginger (chopped)
  • ½ onion (cubed)
  • 1 green bell pepper (cubed)
  • ¼ cup roasted peanuts
  • Spring onion greens (for garnish)
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Marinate the Chicken
  • Mix chicken with soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, cornflour, salt, and pepper.
  • Let it marinate for 15–20 minutes.
🔸 Step 2: Prepare the Sauce
  • In a small bowl, combine soy sauces, vinegar, hoisin, sugar, sesame oil, cornflour, and water.
  • Stir well and set aside.
🔸 Step 3: Stir-Fry
  • Heat oil in a wok or pan over high heat.
  • Add dried red chilies and Sichuan peppercorns (optional). Stir-fry briefly.
  • Add garlic and ginger. Sauté until aromatic.
  • Add marinated chicken and stir-fry for 4–5 minutes until almost cooked.
  • Add onion and bell pepper. Stir-fry for 2 minutes (keep them crisp).
  • Pour in the sauce and stir until it thickens and coats everything.
  • Add roasted peanuts. Toss well to combine.
🔸 Step 4: Finish
  • Garnish with spring onion greens.
  • Serve hot with steamed rice, fried rice, or noodles.

🍯 Sesame Chicken Recipe

🧂 Ingredients
🐔 For the Chicken:
  • 500g boneless chicken (breast or thighs), cubed
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • ½ tsp baking powder (optional – for extra crispiness)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • Oil – for deep frying
🍯 For the Sesame Sauce:
  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 2 tbsp vinegar (white or rice vinegar)
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar (or honey)
  • 2 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • 1 tsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • 1 tsp cornflour + 2 tbsp water (slurry)
  • ¼ cup water
🌱 For Garnish:
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds
  • Spring onion greens (chopped, optional)
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Marinate and Coat Chicken
  • In a bowl, mix chicken, soy sauce, egg, salt, cornflour, maida, and baking powder.
  • Ensure chicken is well coated. Marinate for 10–15 minutes.
🔸 Step 2: Fry the Chicken
  • Heat oil in a deep pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  • Fry chicken pieces in batches until golden brown and crispy.
  • Drain on paper towels.
🔸 Step 3: Make the Sesame Sauce
  • In a bowl, combine soy sauce, ketchup, vinegar, sugar (or honey), sesame oil, and water.
  • In a pan, heat 1 tbsp oil. Add garlic and ginger; sauté for 30 seconds.
  • Pour in the sauce mix and simmer for 1–2 minutes.
  • Add cornflour slurry and stir till sauce thickens and turns glossy.
🔸 Step 4: Combine and Garnish
  • Add fried chicken to the sauce. Toss until evenly coated.
  • Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds and spring onions (optional).
  • Serve hot with rice or noodles.

🍜 Chow Mein Recipe (Vegetable or Chicken)

🧂 Ingredients
🍝 For the Noodles:
  • 200g chow mein or hakka noodles
  • Water + salt + 1 tsp oil (for boiling)
🥕 For Stir-Fry Vegetables:
  • 1 tbsp oil (sesame or vegetable)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (finely chopped)
  • ½ onion (sliced)
  • ½ cup cabbage (shredded)
  • ½ cup carrot (julienned)
  • ½ cup bell pepper (sliced)
  • 2 spring onions (green and white separated)
Optional Protein:
  • 150g boneless chicken or tofu
  • 1 tsp soy sauce + salt + pepper (for marinating)
🥣 For the Sauce:
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili sauce or sriracha (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tbsp vinegar (white or rice vinegar)
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup (optional, for slight sweetness)
  • ½ tsp sugar
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Prepare the Noodles
  • Boil noodles in salted water until 90% cooked (al dente).
  • Drain and rinse under cold water.
  • Toss with 1 tsp oil to prevent sticking. Set aside.
🔸 Step 2: Cook the Chicken (Optional)
  • Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or pan.
  • Stir-fry sliced chicken with salt, pepper, and soy sauce until cooked. Set aside.
🔸 Step 3: Stir-Fry the Vegetables
  • Heat oil in a large wok over high heat.
  • Add garlic, ginger, and spring onion whites. Sauté for 30 seconds.
  • Add onion, cabbage, carrot, and bell pepper.
  • Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes. Keep veggies crunchy.
  • Add cooked chicken or tofu if using.
🔸 Step 4: Add Noodles and Sauces
  • Add the boiled noodles to the pan.
  • Pour in soy sauce, chili sauce, vinegar, ketchup, sugar, salt, and pepper.
  • Toss everything quickly over high heat until combined.
🔸 Step 5: Finish and Serve
  • Garnish with spring onion greens.
  • Serve hot with chili vinegar or soy dip on the side.

🍟🌶️ Crispy Chili Potatoes Recipe

🧂 Ingredients
🥔 For the Crispy Potatoes:
  • 3 medium potatoes (peeled, cut into fingers or wedges)
  • 2 tbsp cornflour
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • ½ tsp red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water – as needed to make batter
  • Oil – for deep frying
🍛 For the Chili Sauce:
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (slit or chopped)
  • ¼ cup onion (sliced)
  • ¼ cup capsicum (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp red chili sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp vinegar
  • ½ tsp sugar (optional)
  • 1 tsp cornflour + 3 tbsp water (slurry)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Spring onion greens (for garnish)
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Prep and Fry Potatoes
  • Soak potato fingers in cold water for 10–15 mins. Drain and pat dry.
  • In a bowl, mix cornflour, maida, chili powder, salt, and a little water to make a thick batter.
  • Coat the potato fingers in the batter.
  • Heat oil and deep fry the potatoes until golden brown and crispy.
  • For extra crispness: cool for 5 mins, then re-fry on high flame for 1 minute.
🔸 Step 2: Prepare the Chili Sauce
  • Heat 1 tbsp oil in a wok or pan.
  • Add chopped garlic, ginger, and green chili. Sauté for 30 seconds.
  • Add sliced onion and capsicum. Stir-fry for 1–2 mins on high flame.
  • Add soy sauce, chili sauce, ketchup, vinegar, sugar. Mix well.
  • Add cornflour slurry and cook until sauce thickens and becomes glossy.
🔸 Step 3: Toss & Serve
  • Add fried potatoes to the sauce. Toss well to coat.
  • Garnish with chopped spring onion greens.
  • Serve immediately as a starter or side dish.

🍗 General Tso’s Chicken Recipe

🧂 Ingredients
🐓 For the Chicken:
  • 500g boneless chicken (breast or thigh), cubed
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 3 tbsp all-purpose flour (maida)
  • ¼ tsp baking soda (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Oil – for deep frying
🥣 For the Sauce:
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp hoisin sauce (optional)
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar (or white vinegar)
  • 2 tbsp brown sugar (or white sugar)
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp cornflour + 3 tbsp water (slurry)
  • ¼ cup water
🍳 For Stir-Fry:
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger (finely chopped)
  • 4–5 dried red chilies (broken)
  • 2 spring onions (white for stir-fry, greens for garnish)
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Marinate and Fry the Chicken
  • In a bowl, mix chicken, egg, soy sauce, salt, pepper, cornflour, maida, and baking soda.
  • Heat oil in a pan and deep-fry chicken in batches until golden brown and crispy.
  • Drain on a paper towel. Set aside.
🔸 Step 2: Make the Sauce
  • In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, hoisin, vinegar, ketchup, sugar, sesame oil, chili flakes, water, and cornflour slurry.
  • Set the sauce aside.
🔸 Step 3: Stir-Fry and Combine
  • Heat 1 tbsp oil in a clean wok.
  • Add garlic, ginger, red chilies, and white spring onions. Sauté for 30 seconds.
  • Pour in the sauce. Stir and cook until it thickens and turns glossy.
  • Add fried chicken and toss to coat well.
🔸 Step 4: Serve
  • Garnish with spring onion greens and sesame seeds (optional).
  • Serve hot with steamed rice or fried rice.

🍲 Hot and Sour Soup Recipe

🧂 Ingredients
🥕 For the Base:
  • 1 tbsp oil (preferably sesame or vegetable)
  • 1 tsp ginger (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp garlic (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional, chopped)
  • ¼ cup carrot (julienned)
  • ¼ cup cabbage (shredded)
  • ¼ cup bell pepper/capsicum (thinly sliced)
  • 2 tbsp spring onion (white part)
  • 3 cups vegetable stock or water
For Chicken Version:
  • Add ½ cup shredded cooked chicken
🥣 For Flavoring:
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar (white or rice vinegar)
  • 1 tbsp red chili sauce
  • 1 tsp green chili sauce (optional)
  • ½ tbsp tomato ketchup (optional)
  • ¼ tsp black pepper powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
🥄 For Thickening:
  • 1 tbsp cornflour + 2 tbsp water (corn slurry)
🌿 For Garnish:
  • Spring onion greens (chopped)
  • Crushed chili flakes (optional)
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Stir-Fry Vegetables
  • Heat oil in a wok or deep pan.
  • Add ginger, garlic, green chili, and spring onion whites. Sauté for 30 seconds.
  • Add carrots, cabbage, and bell peppers. Stir-fry for 2–3 minutes.
🔸 Step 2: Add Liquids and Seasonings
  • Pour in stock or water.
  • Add soy sauce, vinegar, chili sauces, ketchup (if using), salt, sugar, and pepper.
  • Bring to a boil and simmer for 5–6 minutes.
  • For chicken version: Add shredded chicken and simmer for 2–3 more minutes.
🔸 Step 3: Thicken the Soup
  • Mix cornflour and water to form a slurry.
  • Slowly stir it into the soup while boiling.
  • Cook for another 1–2 minutes until thickened.
🔸 Step 4: Garnish and Serve
  • Turn off heat and garnish with spring onion greens and chili flakes.
  • Serve hot as a comforting soup starter or light meal.

🥟 Dumplings Recipe (Veg & Chicken Options)

🧂 Ingredients
🫓 For the Dumpling Dough:
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ¾ cup warm water (adjust as needed)
🥬 For Vegetable Filling:
  • 1 cup cabbage (shredded)
  • ½ cup carrot (grated)
  • ½ cup capsicum (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp spring onions (chopped)
  • 1 tsp garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • ½ tsp vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste
🍗 For Chicken Filling (Optional):
  • 250g minced chicken
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp spring onion (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp onion (finely chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Make the Dough
  • In a bowl, mix flour and salt.
  • Gradually add warm water to knead a smooth, firm dough.
  • Cover and let rest for 20–30 minutes.
🔸 Step 2: Prepare the Filling

🥕 Vegetable Filling:

  • Heat 1 tsp oil in a pan. Sauté garlic, ginger, and veggies for 2–3 minutes.
  • Add soy sauce, vinegar, salt, and pepper. Cook until dry.
  • Let the mixture cool before stuffing.

🍗 Chicken Filling:

  • Mix raw minced chicken with soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, onion, and seasonings.
  • Use as is — no need to pre-cook.
🔸 Step 3: Shape the Dumplings
  • Divide dough into small balls and roll into thin 3-inch circles.
  • Place 1 tbsp of filling in the center of each wrapper.
  • Fold and seal using pleats, half-moon, or pinch styles. Seal edges with water if needed.
🔸 Step 4: Cook the Dumplings

🥟 Steamed Dumplings:

  • Place in a greased steamer and steam for 10–12 minutes until translucent.

🥟 Pan-Fried Dumplings (Potstickers):

  • Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pan. Fry dumplings until golden on the bottom.
  • Add ¼ cup water, cover, and steam for 5–6 minutes until water evaporates.

🥟 Boiled Dumplings:

  • Boil in salted water until they float (about 4–5 mins).
🍽️ Serve Hot
  • Serve with soy sauce, chili oil, or vinegar dip on the side.

🌶️🍛 Mapo Tofu Recipe (Authentic Style)

🧂 Ingredients
🥣 For the Main Dish:
  • 400g soft tofu (silken or medium-firm), cubed
  • 200g minced pork or chicken (optional for veg version)
  • 2 tbsp oil (peanut or vegetable oil)
  • 2–3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1-inch ginger (minced)
  • 2 spring onions (white part for cooking, greens for garnish)
  • 1½ tbsp doubanjiang (Sichuan chili bean paste)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp fermented black beans (optional, mashed)
  • 1 tsp chili oil or red chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 tsp sugar (optional)
  • ½ cup stock (vegetable or chicken) or water
  • 1 tsp Sichuan peppercorns (toasted and crushed)
  • 1 tsp cornflour + 2 tbsp water (for slurry)
👨‍🍳 Instructions
🔸 Step 1: Prepare the Tofu
  • Bring salted water to a gentle boil.
  • Gently boil tofu cubes for 2–3 minutes to firm up.
  • Drain carefully and set aside.
🔸 Step 2: Make the Spicy Meat Base
  • Heat oil in a wok or deep pan.
  • Sauté garlic, ginger, and spring onion whites until aromatic.
  • Add minced pork or chicken and stir-fry until fully cooked.
  • Add doubanjiang and cook for 1–2 minutes.
  • Add fermented black beans (if using), soy sauce, chili oil or flakes, and sugar.
🔸 Step 3: Add Tofu and Simmer
  • Carefully slide in tofu cubes.
  • Add stock or water and simmer for 5–6 minutes on low flame.
  • Add crushed Sichuan peppercorns for heat and aroma.
🔸 Step 4: Thicken the Sauce
  • Prepare cornflour slurry.
  • Gently stir it into the simmering mixture.
  • Cook for 1–2 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the tofu.
🔸 Step 5: Garnish and Serve
  • Garnish with spring onion greens.
  • Serve hot with steamed jasmine rice or plain white rice.
